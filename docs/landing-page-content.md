### Strategy 4: Mean Reversion
**Icon:** üìà
**Full Name:** Mean Reversion
**Risk Level:** High
**Complexity:** Advanced
**Timeframe:** 1-4 hours
**Expected Return:** 25-50% annually
**Max Drawdown:** 25-35%
**Win Rate:** 60-65%

**Description:**
```
Exploits the tendency of prices to return to their average after extreme
movements. Buys oversold assets and sells overbought ones, profiting from
price corrections.
```

**How It Works:**
```
Bot calculates statistical average (mean) price over a period and identifies
when current price deviates significantly. Enters counter-trend positions
expecting price to revert to the mean.
```

**Best For:**
- Experienced traders
- Markets with established price ranges
- Those who understand statistical analysis
- Traders with strict risk management

**Supported Pairs:**
Liquid pairs with stable volatility patterns

**Key Concepts:**
- Bollinger Bands
- Standard deviation
- Overbought/oversold levels
- Statistical mean calculation

**Warning:**
```
Mean reversion works best in ranging markets. In strong trends, "buying the
dip" can lead to continued losses. Strict stop-losses are essential.
```

### Feature 1: Mandatory Stop-Loss
**Icon:** üõ°Ô∏è
**Title:** `Automatic Stop-Loss`

**Description:**
```
Every bot requires a stop-loss setting before deployment. If your position
loses the specified percentage, the bot automatically closes it to prevent
further losses.
```

**How It Works:**
- Set maximum acceptable loss per trade (e.g., 5%)
- Bot monitors all positions in real-time
- Automatically exits when stop-loss is hit
- Prevents emotional decision-making

**Example:**
```
If you set a 10% stop-loss on a $1,000 position, the bot will automatically
sell if the value drops to $900, limiting your loss to $100.
```

---

### Feature 2: Position Size Limits
**Icon:** üìä
**Title:** `Position Size Controls`

**Description:**
```
Limit how much of your capital can be used in a single trade. This ensures
diversification and prevents over-concentration in one position.
```

**Default Settings:**
- Maximum 25% of capital per position
- Adjustable from 10-50%
- Recommended: 20% for beginners

**Why This Matters:**
```
Even if one trade goes wrong, you still have 75% of your capital protected
to trade another day.
```

### Question 1: Is my money safe?
**Answer:**
```
Yes. We never hold or have access to your funds. You connect to exchanges
using API keys with trading-only permissions (no withdrawal rights). Your
capital stays in your exchange account at all times. We simply execute trades
on your behalf using your API keys.

Additionally, all data is encrypted, we're SOC 2 compliant, and we use
industry-standard security practices.
```

---

### Question 2: Do I need to code?
**Answer:**
```
No coding required. Our AI guides you through the entire process using simple
questions and plain English. You'll review everything in an easy-to-understand
dashboard before deployment.

If you want to customize advanced parameters later, you can, but it's entirely
optional.
```

---

### Question 3: What's paper trading?
**Answer:**
```
Paper trading (also called simulated trading) lets you test your bot with
virtual money before risking real capital. You start with $10,000 in virtual
balance, and the bot executes trades in real-time against real market prices
- but with fake money.

This lets you see exactly how your bot performs without any risk. When you're
confident, you can switch to live trading with one click.
```

---

- Position sizes
- Daily loss limits

Everything is adjustable through a simple dashboard interface.
